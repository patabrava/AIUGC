This Privacy Policy explains how Lippe Lift Studio handles account, content, and publishing data when users create and share original short-form videos.

1. Data We Process

The Studio stores workflow data such as post status, scripts, captions, generated video URLs, publishing settings, selected social networks, and operational logs needed to show progress and diagnose failures.

2. TikTok Account Data

When a user connects TikTok, the Studio stores the TikTok account identifier, display name, avatar URL, granted scopes, access token, refresh token, and token expiry timestamps. Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored through the configured encrypted account persistence path and are used only to query creator information, upload approved video content, and refresh publish status.

3. Video And Publishing Data

Before publishing, users can preview the video and edit metadata. When the user confirms posting, the Studio sends the approved video and selected post settings to the chosen social platform. Publish job identifiers, provider status, and error messages are retained so users can understand whether a post is processing, published, awaiting action, or failed.

4. Disconnecting And Deletion

Users can disconnect connected social accounts from the Studio. Disconnecting removes the stored provider connection for future publishing. Requests to delete retained workflow or publishing records can be sent to the Studio operator responsible for the workspace.
